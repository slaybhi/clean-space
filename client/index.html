Project Clean-Space

Municipal solid waste management (MSWM) is one of the major environmental problems of Indian cities. Improper management of municipal solid waste (MSW) causes hazards to inhabitants. Various studies reveal that about 90% of MSW is disposed of unscientifically in open dumps and landfills, creating problems to public health and the environment.

The poor waste management system creates an undesirable atmosphere for us, as a community to live in. This is what Clean-Space is out to control with an efficient mapping system that tracks areas with piles of waste around the city with the help of user inputs from all around the community. We help create an easier platform for the community to report its grievances and an efficient way for the muncipalities and NGOs to find and clean out the reported areas

Thus we, Clean-Space make it our responsibility and mission to allow the involvement of all the people in creating a greener and more sustainable future. We believe that each and every person can easily and equally contribute towards this common goal with the help of our project and hopefully create a brighter and happier tomorrow for everyone
